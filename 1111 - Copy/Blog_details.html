Ramadan

Read the text below.

The whiffs of the holy month of Ramadan come upon us, in which we gather the blessings of mercy and forgiveness, and we pray to God to reveal all humanity from the evils of epidemics and diseases. The month of Ramadan is a great annual opportunity for repentance and repentance, changing bad habits, contemplating the Qur’an, sensing the sweetness of prayer and standing, kindness and mercy for the needy and the poor. In the Iftar and Suhoor meals, members of the same family meet at one table and eat the same food to strengthen the bonds of love and strengthen social bonds.

Perhaps you should encourage your child to fast during the month of Ramadan, so that he feels the thrill of victory over himself. Have him listen to the audiobook “Children in Ramadan” or read the following books: “Love in Ramadan,” “The Crescent of Ramadan,” “The Lights of Ramadan,” or “The Month of Ramadan.”
